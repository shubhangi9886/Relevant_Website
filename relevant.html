relevant

Something is relevant if it's appropriate or
connected to the matter at hand. Relevant
things are helpful and on point.

Start learning this word

Relevant things are appropriate and make sense at that particular time.
In the middle of history class, your teacher loves to get relevant
questions: questions that have to do with the material. The teacher won't
be thrilled to get a question about math: that's not relevant. You can also
say a song or movie is relevant if it's connected to current events or
issues people are talking about. When something is relevant, it just fits
what is happening.
